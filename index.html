codesy is a pay-what-you-want market
for the open source community
to encourage coders to fix important bugs.

Add to Chrome Add to Firefox

every day, millions of us coders work around bugs in open-source software.

codesy helps us all get our real work done by boosting incentives to fix those bugs in open-source software.

when you see a bug, click the codesy button, and answer two questions:

  1. How much will you pitch in for a fix?
  2. How much do you need to fix it yourself?

We'll combine all the offers until they match an asking price, then notify the matching coder to fix the bug and collect their earnings.
